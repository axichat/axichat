Axichat is a cutting-edge email and XMPP client that is fully open source with 1st party push notifications (no FCM ever) and no 3rd party dependencies.

You can self-host your own email + XMPP server for Axichat by following the steps at https://github.com/axichat/selfhost.

Feature highlights:
- Unified inbox for chat + email side by side.
- Group chats and per-conversation settings.
- Quick quote-reply, reactions, and delivery/read receipts.
- Rich attachments and inline previews.
- Fast search across chat, mail, and calendar.
- Collaborative calendar with reminders and tasks.
- Natural-language scheduling (no AI required).
- Availability sharing to schedule meetings faster.
- Critical path and agenda focus tools.
- Smart notifications with per-chat controls.
- Guest mode for offline planning.
- First-party push notifications and offline sync.
- Works without Google, Firebase, or FCM.

Why Axichat?
- Tools matter: communication should feel like a clean desk, not a cluttered inbox.
- Time matters: Axichat reduces context-switching with chat-style email and integrated planning.
- Collaboration matters: calendar, availability, and messaging stay in one place.

What Axichat does:

Best chat interface:
- Read and send chat and email in one conversation-centric UI.
- Reach people on regular email providers (for example Gmail, Outlook, Tuta) while keeping your workflow in Axichat.
- Use advanced XMPP features when both sides support them.

Best calendar in the world:
- Type tasks and events in natural language and schedule quickly.
- Keep unscheduled items in an inbox until you place them.
- Plan with drag-and-drop, resize, copy/paste, and batch editing.
- Track reminders, deadlines, priorities, and recurring plans.
- Use guest mode for local planning without account setup.

Privacy and independence:
- No trackers.
- No ads.
- No data selling.
- No centralized lock-in.
- Open protocols and open-source code.

Android permissions explained:
- INTERNET: send and receive chat/email data.
- POST_NOTIFICATIONS: show message and reminder notifications.
- FOREGROUND_SERVICE and related data-sync permissions: keep reliable background messaging.
- RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: restore background messaging after reboot.
- REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: optional prompt to improve delivery reliability.
- VIBRATE: notification haptics.
