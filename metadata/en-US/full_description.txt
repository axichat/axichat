Axichat is an open source email (SMTP) and chat (XMPP) client with first-party push notifications (without FCM) and a conversation-focused interface.

Get your.name@axi.im now.

For additional privacy and security, run your own server by following the self-hosting guide at https://github.com/axichat/selfhost.

Feature highlights:
- First-party push notifications and offline sync
- Transform traditional email into instant messenger style
- Sync across all your devices (mobile and desktop)
- Group chats
- Avatars
- Easy forwarding and replying
- Emoji reactions
- Delivery and read receipts
- Typing indicators
- Attachments
- Pinned messages
- Stream management with automatic reconnect to stop messages dropping
- Easy drag-and-drop calendar
- Natural-language scheduling without AI
- Availability sharing that shows overlaps before you schedule
- One-tap add-to-calendar from simple text messages
- Guest mode for offline planning
- Works without Google, Firebase, and FCM

<strong>Note:</strong> Axichat is still under active development, so things may break.

Please report bugs at https://github.com/axichat/axichat/issues or GitLab.
